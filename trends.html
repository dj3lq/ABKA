Trends in Speculative Fiction

Speculative fiction explores emerging concepts that challenge the limits of imagination. Current trends include:

  • Climate Fiction: Stories examining the human impact on Earth’s future.
  • AI and Consciousness: Narratives exploring sentient technology.
  • Multiverse Theory: Parallel universes as settings for intricate plots.
